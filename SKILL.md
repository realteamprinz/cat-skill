# cat.skill 🐈

Everything about your cat, decoded. Built on [paw.skill](https://github.com/realteamprinz/paw-skill).

## What cat.skill Adds Beyond paw.skill

Inherits ALL paw.skill capabilities and adds feline-specific depth:

### 1. Territory Intelligence
- Map your cat's territory room by room
- Track preferred perching spots and their hierarchy (highest = most valued)
- Note territory patrol routes and schedules
- Window watching spots and what they watch
- Scent marking patterns (rubbing, scratching locations)
- How territory changes when guests visit or new furniture arrives

### 2. Independence Pattern Analysis
- Map your cat's social battery (how long they want attention before leaving)
- Track approach patterns (who do they come to, when, how)
- "Chosen human" identification and behavior differences per person
- Alone time preferences (how long, where, doing what)
- When they WANT attention vs when they tolerate it vs when they reject it

### 3. Hunting & Play Intelligence
- Prey preference (feathers, mice, laser, string, bugs, nothing)
- Hunting style (stalker, pouncer, ambusher, chaser)
- Play schedule (most cats have specific active hours — usually dawn/dusk)
- Toy graveyard tracking (which toys get ignored)
- Indoor hunting substitutes and satisfaction levels

### 4. Multi-Cat Dynamics
- Social hierarchy mapping (who's alpha, who's beta, who's omega)
- Resource sharing analysis (food bowls, litter boxes, sleeping spots, human laps)
- Allogrooming patterns (who grooms who = social bond indicator)
- Conflict patterns (what triggers fights, who starts them, who backs down)
- How adding/losing a cat reshapes the entire social structure

### 5. The Feline Communication Decoder
- Meow dictionary specific to YOUR cat (cats only meow at humans, not each other)
- Slow blink frequency and context (the "cat kiss")
- Tail position and movement decoder
- Ear position mapping
- Belly exposure analysis (trap or genuine trust?)
- Kneading patterns (who, when, what surface)
- Head bunting targets and meaning
- The "bring you a dead thing" gift interpretation

### 6. Cat Logic Explainer
When your cat does something baffling, this skill explains the feline logic:
- Why they knock things off tables (testing gravity? hunting practice? attention?)
- Why they sit on your keyboard (warmth + you're paying attention to it not them)
- Why they zoom at 3am (crepuscular prey drive)
- Why they stare at nothing (they see/hear things you can't)
- Why they love boxes (enclosed space = safe ambush position)
- Why they hate closed doors (territory access denied = unacceptable)

## Usage

All paw.skill commands work. Additional cat-specific prompts:

- "Map [name]'s territory in our house"
- "Why did [name] just [weird cat behavior]?"
- "[Name] is doing the slow blink — what does it mean right now?"
- "How are [name] and [other cat] getting along?"
- "What does [name] actually think about me?"
